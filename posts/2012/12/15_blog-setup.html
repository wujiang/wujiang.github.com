Blog Setup

  •  
  • Score My Mutt 

This blog is powered by o-blog, which converts Org files into static html pages. As an Emacs + Org user, o-blog is the best blog tool so far to myself. It allows me to use the all the features from Org, at the same time provides a lot of cool features.

Setting up the basic page is relatively straight forward. Let's starts from our "Hello World!" example. 

 1:  #+TITLE: Hello World Blog
 2:  #+DESCRIPTION: How to create a basic blog using o-blog
 3:  
 4:  #+STARTUP: logdone
 5:  
 6:  #+TEMPLATE_DIR: templates
 7:  #+URL: http://helloworld.blog
 8:  
 9:  #+DISQUS: hello-world-demo
10:  #+FILENAME_SANITIZER: ob-sanitize-string
11:  #+POST_SORTER: ob-sort-posts-by-date
12:  
13:  * Home Page
14:  ,  :PROPERTIES:
15:  ,  :PAGE: index.html
16:  ,  :TEMPLATE: blog_static_no_title.html
17:  ,  :END:
18:  
19:  #+begin_o_blog_row 8
20:  
21:  #+begin_o_blog_hero_unit
22:  #+HTML: <h1><lisp>(ob:blog-title BLOG)</lisp></h1>
23:  #+end_o_blog_hero_unit
24:  
25:  #+o_blog_row_column 4
26:  
27:  #+HTML: <div class="hero-unit" style="font-size: 100%;">
28:  
29:  ,/icon-github-sign icon-white/ github
30:  
31:  #+HTML: </div>
32:  
33:  #+end_o_blog_row
34:  
35:  #+begin_o_blog_row 5
36:  ** Hello World
37:  ,    Welcome to our =Hello World= demo site.
38:  
39:  #+end_o_blog_row
40:  
41:  * Blog Details
42:  ** Copyright
43:  ,   :PROPERTIES:
44:  ,   :SNIPPET: t
45:  ,   :END:
46:  
47:  ** About
48:  ,  :PROPERTIES:
49:  ,  :SNIPPET: t
50:  ,  :END:
51:  
52:  ,   =Hello World= demo site using o-blog
53:   
54:  ** Navigation
55:  ,  :PROPERTIES:
56:  ,  :SNIPPET: t
57:  ,  :END:
58:  
59:  ** Navigation Footer
60:  ,  :PROPERTIES:
61:  ,  :SNIPPET:t
62:  ,  :END:
63:  
64:  ** Articles by tags
65:  ,  :PROPERTIES:
66:  ,  :PAGE: tags.html
67:  ,  :TEMPLATE: blog_post-by-tags.html
68:  ,   :END:
69:  
70:  * Posts
71:  ** DONE Hello World                         :demo:hello_world:
72:  ,   CLOSED: [2012-12-15 Sat 15:35]
73:  ,   :PROPERTIES:
74:  ,   :END:
75:  
76:  ,   This is our =Hello World= example by using o-blog.
77:  ,   It's required to insert an empty line between =:END:= and the content
78:  ,   of the post. A post only get published when it's state cycle changed
79:  ,   from =TODO= to =DONE=  which is a great feature which allows us to
80:  ,   keep our in-progress post private until it's done.
81:
